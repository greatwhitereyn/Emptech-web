ABOUT US

How Our Journey Started?

It all started with her.

At first, I never really paid much attention to flowers. But when I found out how much Alexis loved them—how her eyes lit up at every bouquet, how she admired every petal, every color, every scent—I knew I had to do something special.

So, I started making handmade bouquets just for her. At first, it was just a small gesture, a way to show how much I appreciated her. But the more I arranged flowers, the more I realized… I was falling in love—not just with her, but with the art of floristry itself.

Seeing how happy it made her, I wanted to share that same joy with others. And that’s how LexisBloom was born—a florist shop inspired by love, dedicated to creating moments of happiness, one bloom at a time.
