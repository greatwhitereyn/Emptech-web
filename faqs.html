Frequently Asked Questions

What types of flowers do you offer?

We offer a wide selection of flowers including roses, lilies, tulips, daisies, orchids, and many seasonal varieties.

Do you offer flower delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery in the local area and next-day delivery for other regions.

How can I place an order for flowers?

You can place an order through our website, by phone, or by visiting our store directly.

Can I customize a bouquet or arrangement?

Absolutely! You can choose the flowers, colors, and styles that best suit your needs, and our florists will create the perfect arrangement for you.

Do you offer gift items along with flowers?

Yes, we offer a selection of gifts including chocolates, teddy bears, and vases that can be added to any floral arrangement.

Are the flowers fresh?

We receive fresh deliveries every day to ensure that our flowers are of the highest quality.

Do you offer flowers for events?

Yes, we provide flowers for weddings, corporate events, parties, and other special occasions. Contact us to discuss your event details.

How should I care for my flowers?

Each flower type may have different care instructions, but generally, keep your flowers in water, change the water regularly, and avoid direct sunlight.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

We offer seasonal promotions and discounts on larger orders. Be sure to sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on current offers.
